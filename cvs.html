The verdict system

Traffic light decisions. No ambiguity.

Every candidate lands in one of three categories based on their total score.

Shortlisted

Score 80 or above. Meets experience requirement and most required skills. Move to interview.

Review

Score between 50 and 79. Partial match — worth a manual look before deciding.

Rejected

Score below 50. Does not meet the minimum threshold for this role.

What's under the hood

Built for real HR workflows.

Every detail was designed around what actually matters in a hiring process.

PII redaction before processing

Email addresses and phone numbers are automatically redacted before the CV is analysed. The scoring is based on skills and experience only — no personal data is sent anywhere.

Batch processing — any number of CVs

Upload 1 CV or 100 in the same run. The system processes each one independently and returns a single ranked CSV with all candidates.

Evidence-backed extraction

Every matched skill includes the exact sentence from the CV that proves it. Your HR team can verify any decision in seconds — no black box.

Output ready for immediate use

The CSV shortlist opens directly in Excel or Google Sheets. Sort by score, filter by decision, share with the hiring manager — zero reformatting needed.
